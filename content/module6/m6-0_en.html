Introduction

In order to find solutions to the new challenges that Canada faces, government departments and other organizations are encouraged to share their knowledge and expertise with each other. Such collaboration among departments and other parts of government is invaluable because it enables the government to achieve its various mandates.

Upon completion of this module, you should be able to:

  • Understand the importance of interdepartmental collaboration to major initiatives;
  • Distinguish among the different levels of government in which collaboration may occur.
