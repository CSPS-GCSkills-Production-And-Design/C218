Conclusion

In this module, you saw how, as a public servant, you may be asked to work with other departments and levels of government. You play a vital role in ensuring the machinery of government is able to deliver on important initiatives that affect every area of Canada.

In this course, you were shown the importance of a foundational understanding of Canada's non-partisan federal public service to the democratically elected government of the day.
